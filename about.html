I'm a Realtor with Pro Realty Group, working under associate broker Billy Johair, focused entirely on Dearborn and Dearborn Heights. I don't cover the whole metro — I cover these two cities well.

Outside of real estate, I teach and guide Shia Muslim students, from 5th and 6th graders up through high school, and help build out the Islamic curriculum they learn from. That work shapes how I show up as an agent too — a lot of patience, a lot of listening before advising, and a genuine stake in this community beyond the transaction.

When someone works with me, they're not getting handed off to a transaction coordinator they've never spoken to. I'm the one answering the phone, writing the offer, and standing at the closing table.
